    A brush handle you are not required to grip with your thumb and with only two fingers have the ability to scrub the hardest and get to the most difficult parts of your body to clean.

    • Dropping a back brush while hands are wet and soapy.

    • The inability to control and apply pressure while washing the back.

    There are a few ways our invention helps.

    When you turn the head of the brush, it creates an unbelievable ability to produce force on the back and optimizes control and ability with less effort.
