    • About Halifax Brush Handle Inc.

    We are a small family business that was formed to bring our ideas for new inventions to the market place.

    First, the idea is recorded in our new invention log book.

    Then it's investigated to see if it would qualify as a new invention, customer base and its marketing possibilities.

    Third, we manufacture the prototypes.

    After careful consideration and consultation with our lawyers and all the proper channels regarding a new invention, we bring it to the world.

    Our first invention - The Halifax Brush Handle - was invented by the President of Halifax Brush Handle Inc. who himself, suffers from a disability and on his journey for a more relaxing and comfortable experience in personal care products, dreamed and designed "The Halifax Brush Handle".
